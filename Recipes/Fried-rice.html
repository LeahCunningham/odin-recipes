Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Capscicum
  • Carrot
  • Egg
  • Spring onion
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables and cook in oil a pan at high heat
  2. Add rice and stir
  3. Add crushed garlic and soy sauce
  4. Combine until all mixed and heated through
  5. Remove from pan and serve in a bowl
  6. Fry an egg and put it on top of the fried rice
