Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Corn
  • Avocado
  • Spring onion
  • Taco chili mix
  • Chipoltle sauce
  • Black beans
  • Tinned tomato
  • Oil
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Chop spring onion, and slice avocado
  3. Add corn, beans, tinned tomato and taco spice mix in a pot and stir over medium heat
  4. Spoon rice into serving bowls, top with the Mexican mix and place avocado slices on top
